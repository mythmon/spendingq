This is a site for calculating and graphing your Spending Quotient which is a way to track your macro ability. You'll start by logging in the top right. Then go the "My Graph" and you can enter your average unspent resources and collection rate from the score screen. This will plot a single point on the graph, once you get a couple points plotted you'll be able to see if you are getting better (going up) or worse (going down) at macro!
